Education

About the Program

At Curio, we offer an engaging program in software development, where you'll master the art of creating websites and applications. Our locations in Breda and Roosendaal are the ideal places to acquire these skills. In the initial years of the program, you'll spend your time at school, where you'll learn the fundamentals of the field. Then, you'll take it a step further by interning in the field four days a week while returning to the classroom for further depth one day a week.

×

Accelerated Program from 4 Years to 3 Years

Are you a fast learner and want to earn your diploma more quickly? That's possible! Our program offers the option to shorten the standard four-year route to just three years. If you already have some prior knowledge of topics covered in the program, you can switch to the accelerated track after the first half-year. Here, you'll have the opportunity to intensify the program with one additional subject and more weekly hours to speed up your learning. The decision to join the accelerated track is made in consultation with teachers and based on your performance during practical assignments and assessments.

Program and Blocks System

Our program is divided into seven blocks, each lasting half a year. During this period, you'll delve deep into various themes and topics. In two of these blocks, namely Block D and Block G, you'll have the opportunity to gain practical experience through internships. If, for any reason, you don't successfully complete a block, you won't have to redo an entire year; instead, you can get back to it in the following year right away. This flexible system allows you to learn and grow at your own pace.
